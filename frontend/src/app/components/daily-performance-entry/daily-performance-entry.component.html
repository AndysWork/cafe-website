Instructions

  • In/Out Time: Enter the staff's clock-in and clock-out times. Working hours will be calculated automatically.
  • Orders: Total orders should equal or exceed good orders + bad orders.
  • Refund Recovery: Enter the amount recovered from staff for order refunds (if applicable).
  • Fill Defaults: Click the magic wand icon to fill default times (9 AM - 6 PM).
  • Save Individual: Click the check icon to save a single staff entry.
  • Save All: Click "Save All Changes" button at the top to save all edited entries at once.
  • Data Persistence: Previously entered data will load automatically when you select a date.
